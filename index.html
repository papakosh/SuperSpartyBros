Game Description:

A 2D platformer where you must collect coins, avoid enemies,
and go for the Rose! Then, at the very end defeat Nemesis.


Game Controls:

Keyboard/Mouse = WASD or arrow keys to navigate,
SPACE to jump, and MOUSE 1 to shoot.

Android On-screen = Touch pad to navigate, JUMP button to jump,
and FIRE button to shoot.
